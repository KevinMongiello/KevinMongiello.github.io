About Us

Our Mission

Helping our customers since 2023

Golden Sun Solar Services was started by Christopher Pannella, an avid outdoorsman with a deep passion for renewable energy. He has been in solar for over four years working for big name companies in Colorado and gaining a vast wealth of experience along the way.

After seeing that some people liked to cut corners & use shady business practices to sell jobs, Chris decided that he could do it better. Golden Sun is named after the magnificent sunsets he fell in love with working the late evenings on rooftops in the solar industry.
